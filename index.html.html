Returns Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Our Returns Window

We accept return requests within 14 days of your order being delivered. Items must be unused, unworn and in their original condition with all tags attached.

Eligible for Return

  • Items received in a damaged or faulty condition
  • Items that are significantly different from their description
  • Wrong item received

Not Eligible for Return

  • Items that have been worn, washed or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Change of mind purchases
  • Items purchased during a sale or at a discounted price

How to Request a Return

To start a return, message us on Instagram at @xclusivelines with your order number and reason for return. We'll review your request and get back to you within 24 hours with next steps.

DM us @xclusivelines

Refunds

Once your return is approved and received, we will issue a refund to your XclusiveLine account balance within 3–5 business days. The balance can then be used on future orders or we can arrange a PayPal refund upon request.

Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is faulty or incorrectly sent. We recommend using a tracked shipping service for all returns as we cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit.
